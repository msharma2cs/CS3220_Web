Something Interesting

I feel that the scarcity of interesting things in me is the most interesting point of me. I have often heard people associating mysterious adverb with my name, for only god knows what reason.

Though my close friends are too few in numbers, but they are the only ones to know that I am a very fun loving and crazy person. My introvertness and shyness hides that very impression of me.

Though my body appearance and built may not justify this, but I like running and have even completed few marathons, though mostly the mini ones.

Books are the best companion of my life. I am an avid reader and can finish it off quiet quickly. I have been carrying a desire for personal library room for long. And yes, I do have an addiction for the smell of a fresh printed book.
